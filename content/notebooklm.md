# NotebookLM

- **Link:** https://notebooklm.google/
- **Category:** Tools & Software

## Why is this resource useful?

NotebookLM lets you shove whole books, docs, and resources in and pull out the core concepts fast. Clean, accurate, and grounded in the actual source. You can spin those ideas into mindmaps, quizzes, flashcards, or whatever learning style you're in the mood for, without doing the tedious extraction yourself. It basically collapses weeks of slow reading into a tight, deep understanding you can use immediately.
